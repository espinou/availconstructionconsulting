Property Insurance Claims Consulting

Forensic Construction Consulting provides building construction expertise to ensure that proper work scopes are generated, accurate estimates and costs are submitted, proper repairs are performed and legitimate claims are documented. Our services include detailed damage opinions, causation opinions, and cost to repair evaluations for both building owners and insurance companies to quickly settle the matter.
