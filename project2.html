Water Damage – San Diego, Ca

Issue:

ACC worked extensively with the owner’s legal counsel through this multi-year investigation, legal action, and resolution process. This sophisticated beachfront estate suffered from extensive construction detail failures related to the main house, guest house, pool pavilion, elevated decks and balconies, cabana, and porte-cochère. These seemingly innocuous details actually caused substantial and costly damage.

Resolution:

After an extensive investigation of the property, ACC provided the following services which were instrumental to ultimately prevail against the parties:

  • Building Envelope Evaluation
  • Discovery Review and Support
  • Defect Causation Analysis
  • Photo / Video Documentation
  • Remediation Construction Repair Protocol Development
  • Damage Cost Analysis Reporting
  • Expert Witness Services / Testimony
  • Physical Evidence Removal and Preservation
  • Physical Evidence Storage
  • Building Code Violation Reporting
