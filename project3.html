Owner Representation – San Diego, Ca

Issue:

Craig Kobza served as the homeowner’s representative during the land acquisition, development, and construction phases of their new beachfront home. The discerning homeowners wanted to build a masterpiece that was low maintenance, energy efficient, and could weather the test of time. These world travelers could only move the project forward efficiently with ACC’s committed services.

Resolution:

ACC coordinated and assisted the homeowners with pre-construction and construction-related aspects of the building process. This included aspects from design to completion, including home site selection, design coordination, construction implementation, and approval.

Services provided included:

  • Coordinating and advising on the selection of key team members, including subject matter experts, engineers, architects, general contractors, sub-contractors and interior designers
  • Constant communication with project team members to ensure consistent execution of design
  • Selecting and negotiating contracts with the project team members
  • Identifying and advising on key building material components and systems
  • Conducting frequent site visits to ensure quality control
  • Preparing written and photographic progress updates to the owners
