When we perform our initial site evaluation, we are mindful that any building performance complaint has the potential to rise to the level of litigation.

Our company provides innovative solutions to complex building issues relating to the building envelope – and more. We have provided consulting services for building owners, insurance companies, attorneys, developers and architects for over 15 years. ACC stays at the forefront of the construction industry by keeping up with the latest building science techniques, current building codes and local construction industry standards. Utilizing state of the art technology, such as drones, infrared photography, and minute to minute environmental monitoring, ACC is able to evaluate, diagnose and recommend repairs for some of the most complex construction defects and installation failures.
