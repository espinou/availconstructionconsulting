Dispute Resolution & Litigation Support

Are you currently in a dispute over the construction of your building? Avail Construction Consulting assists in conducting on-site informative meetings with all relevant parties to discuss the building performance concerns. We identify whether the building performance complaints stem from installation, design or product failures. Through the use of controlled and documented analytical and investigative techniques, we are able to identify, collect, and preserve the physical evidence – all while photo documenting the project. ACC is well versed in the various ASTM (American Society for Testing and Materials) standards for evidence preservation and reporting.

Dispute Resolution & Litigation Process

When we perform our initial site evaluation, we are mindful that any building performance complaint has the potential to rise to the level of litigation. Accordingly, our building evaluation process is unmatched, owing to our combined experience in building construction consulting and litigation support.

Our typical process involves the following:

  • Initial interview with the client to define preliminary issues
  • Inspect and Document As-Is Conditions
  • Review of Original Construction Documentation
  • Establish a Timeline of the Original Building Process
  • Limited Initial Sample Testing
  • Detailed Photo Documentation
  • Identify any building code violations
  • Collection and preservation in accordance with ASTM Standards
