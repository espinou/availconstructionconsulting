Building Construction Consulting

Avail Construction Consulting has the knowledge and experience to evaluate and assess your various building concerns during the construction consulting process. We are able to identify and examine building systems in an effort to understand the failure condition, through the use of controlled and documented analytical and investigative techniques – all while photo documenting the project. Our 40+ years of experience in conducting building envelope analyses allows us to provide our clients with a clear path forward.

Building Defect Analysis

Building envelope defects and structural issues can be caused by subpar workmanship, design defects, faulty products or systems reaching the end of their life cycle. These conditions can result in water intrusion, mold, structural damage, and other serious problems.

We stay at the forefront of the construction industry by keeping up with the latest building science techniques, current building codes and local construction industry standards. By utilizing state of the art technology, such as drone and infrared photography and minute to minute environmental monitoring, ACC is able to diagnose and recommend repairs for the most complex construction defects and installation failures.

Our Process

Our building construction consulting process allows us to provide the best service possible to our clients. Our typical process involves the following:

  • Initial interview with the client to define preliminary concerns
  • Document As-Is Conditions
  • Review of Original Construction Documentation
  • Establish a Timeline of the Original Building Process
  • Limited Initial Sample Testing
  • Detailed Photo Documentation
  • Inspect for construction defects that may be causal to the underlying issue
