Avail Construction Consulting

Ascent Construction Consulting (ACC) provides construction consulting services to resolve complex building issues with real-world solutions. Our decades of experience with harsh waterfront environments allow us to quickly identify all types of construction issues. Services include: building envelope analysis, remediation services, repair work protocols with specified building materials, property insurance claims review, building code violation reporting, litigation support – if required and more. Our innovative solutions allow us to provide our clients with a clear path forward.
